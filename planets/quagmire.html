Quagmire

Way down under the ground.

“Echoes of voices through underground tunnels. Researchers in protective gear breaching the surface. Storm of rocks falling onto a dark planet.”

Located extremely close to the system's star, this tidely-locked planet contains few locations for life. On the starside of the planet, the molten lava evaporates and rises into the atmosphere until it cools at higher altitudes and rains down on the spaceside of Quagmire, with much escaping the atmosphere towards neighboring planets. Civilization there exists entirely underground, an impressive sprawl of tunnels and below ground domes designed initially for the researchers who came to explore the massive abundance of Precursor-ruins that previously littered the surface.
