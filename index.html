Far in the future...

The year is 1242 AE (After Exploration) and the woes of earth and Capitalism have traveled with us into space. Our story takes place in Gamma Sector, located far away from the core of the Hegemony, consists of 4 systems filled with Syndicates, mystics, and other folks that live on the fringes. Too far and too small to have the strong arm of the Hegemony truly clutched around its throat, Gamma Sector is run by the corrupt Alliance, a coalition of wealthy planets that have mined the sector for its resources, while keeping within the Hegemony's good graces.
